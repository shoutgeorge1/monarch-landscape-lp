> Why Mulch Matters in the Pacific Northwest

**Benefit Cards (6 total, each with icon + H3 + 1-line description):**

2. **Weed suppression and reduced maintenance costs**

3. **Protection for roots during cold, wet winters**

   Helps maintain consistent soil temperatures for healthier plant growth.

5. **Improved landscape appearance and uniformity**
   Creates a clean, polished, cohesive look across your property.

6. **Prevents erosion in high-rainfall seasons**
   Protects topsoil from washing away during heavy Pacific Northwest rains.
